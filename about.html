About Me

Hello! My name is Trevor.

I grew up in Fort Worth, TX and currently live in San Francisco, CA. The first experience I ever had with anything computer-related was when my dad bought me a Super Nintendo for Christmas, which must have been back in 1994 or so. It was such a surprise to get, I was still very young and had no idea what it was. It spurred a lifelong love of games for me! A few years later, we were able to get a windows PC. I continued playing games, now on the internet and with others, and used applications like AIM and IRC to keep in contact with people. I still love playing games to this day. Some of those old Super Ninendo games are still some of the best games ever created! These days I mostly play Rocket League, Civilization, Call of Duty, or Axie Infinity. Maybe one day I'll have the skills to create my own. :)

One of the other things I've always loved is sports. I started playing when I was pretty young, and I started playing baseball first. I eventually started playing football and basketball as well. As a teenager, I also got into skateboarding! These days the closest thing I do to playing sports is playing fantasy football.

I'm very passionate about music. My mom likes to tell me that she always played really good music when she was pregnant with me and that it seemed like I was dancing. The good tunes continued after I was born, of course, and I can thank her, my dad, and my cousin for helping cultivate my love for music. One of the things I always wish I had done is learned to play an instrument. Maybe after I learn to code, I can take that on as a new hobby. Check out what I've been listening to recently at my last.fm!
